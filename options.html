Autopilot

The autopilot performs a series of actions and repeats them after a certain pause. As soon as you have defined at least 2 actions, the autopilot can be started on the Twitter homepage (you may need to refresh the Twitter page). The Mass Follow, Mass Unfollow, Mass Like and Mass Retweet options defined above do apply.

Watch the demo to see how to set it up

In the free version, the autopilot is limited to 2 actions.
